StampT

Background:

StampT is a travel app concept that gamifies exploration through digital passport stamps. Designed to make travel more immersive and authentic, it rewards users for completing unique, experience-based milestones, like “Tried KBBQ in Seoul” or “Watched a sumo match in Tokyo.” By blending location services, translation tools, and social sharing, StampT transforms travel into a playful and community-driven adventure.

The Challenge:

Many travel apps focus on logistics or statistics, planning itineraries, tracking countries visited, or logging routes - without capturing the essence of lived experiences. StampT addresses this gap by encouraging users to slow down, engage with local culture, and collect stamps tied to meaningful moments rather than destinations alone.
